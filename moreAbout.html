What Is A Classical Baptist Church? By Dr. John S. Waldrip

Classical Baptist Press exists to bless the family of God, but especially Baptists who are committed to Gospel preaching, biblical evangelism, the reality of a sinnerâ€™s depravity, the new birth as a transformative miracle wrought by the Holy Spirit in the life of the sinner who comes to Jesus Christ in response to his exposure to the Gospel message, strongly resisting the tendency to be tossed about by every wind of change and the latest fads, and rejecting the Protestant view of the Church set forth in the Westminster Confession of Faith.

Further, we seek to encourage and educate believers to embrace missionary Baptist convictions, recognizing our Great Commission is rightly obeyed when Christians render service to Christ to, through and from congregations where there is accountability, the administration of the two ordinances, properly administered discipline, and when congregations are involved in starting other Churches to carry out the Great Commission in other places.
